Event Rules & Regulations

Please read the rules and regulations carefully for each event before participating in Colosseum 2k26.

Build the Building

General Rules

  • Each team can have a maximum of 3 members
  • Participants must bring their own materials (specified list will be provided)
  • Time limit: 3 hours for construction
  • All structures must be built on-site during the event
  • Use of pre-built components is strictly prohibited

Materials & Specifications

  • Only approved materials will be allowed (ice cream sticks, glue, thread, etc.)
  • Maximum dimensions: 30cm x 30cm x 30cm
  • Structure must be able to support minimum weight as specified
  • No use of metal, plastic sheets, or commercial building materials

Judging Criteria

  • Structural stability and load-bearing capacity (40%)
  • Innovation and creativity (25%)
  • Adherence to specifications (20%)
  • Time management (15%)

Note: Teams violating any rule will be disqualified immediately. The decision of the judges is final.

Testing an Engineer

General Rules

  • Individual participation only (no teams)
  • Duration: 60 minutes
  • Total questions: 50 (Multiple Choice Questions)
  • No negative marking
  • Use of mobile phones, calculators, or any electronic devices is prohibited

Question Distribution

  • Civil Engineering Fundamentals: 20 questions
  • Structural Analysis: 10 questions
  • Construction Materials: 10 questions
  • General Aptitude: 10 questions

Scoring & Evaluation

  • Each correct answer: +2 marks
  • No marks for unanswered questions
  • In case of tie, time taken will be considered
  • Top 3 participants will be awarded

Note: Participants must report 15 minutes before the event starts. Late entries will not be allowed.

CAD Catalog

General Rules

  • Individual or team participation (max 2 members)
  • Duration: 4 hours
  • Participants must bring their own laptops with CAD software installed
  • Internet access will be provided for reference only
  • All work must be completed on-site

Software & Requirements

  • Allowed software: AutoCAD, Revit, SketchUp, or similar CAD tools
  • Problem statement will be given on the spot
  • Participants must submit both 2D and 3D views
  • Final submission must include drawings and rendered images

Judging Criteria

  • Design accuracy and precision (35%)
  • Innovation and creativity (30%)
  • Technical skills and software proficiency (25%)
  • Presentation quality (10%)

Note: Participants must ensure their laptops are fully charged. Power outlets will be available but limited.

Cube Crusher

General Rules

  • Team event (2-3 members per team)
  • Duration: 2.5 hours
  • Standard concrete cubes will be provided
  • Teams must predict the crushing strength before testing
  • Actual testing will be done using compression testing machine

Procedure

  • Teams will receive concrete cubes of standard dimensions (150mm x 150mm x 150mm)
  • Visual inspection and non-destructive testing allowed
  • Teams must submit their predictions before actual testing
  • Closest prediction to actual strength wins

Judging Criteria

  • Accuracy of prediction (60%)
  • Understanding of material properties (25%)
  • Testing methodology explanation (15%)

Note: Safety equipment (gloves, safety glasses) will be provided. Participants must follow all safety protocols.

Paper Presentation

General Rules

  • Individual or team participation (max 2 members)
  • Presentation duration: 10 minutes (8 min presentation + 2 min Q&A)
  • Topics must be related to Civil Engineering
  • Abstract submission deadline: 7 days before the event
  • Full paper submission: 3 days before the event

Paper Format

  • Abstract: 200-300 words
  • Full paper: Maximum 8 pages (A4 size)
  • Font: Times New Roman, 12pt, 1.5 line spacing
  • Must include: Abstract, Introduction, Methodology, Results, Conclusion, References

Presentation Guidelines

  • PowerPoint presentation required (max 15 slides)
  • Participants must bring their own laptops
  • Projector and screen will be provided
  • Strict time limit - presentation will be stopped after 10 minutes

Judging Criteria

  • Technical content and research quality (40%)
  • Presentation skills and clarity (30%)
  • Innovation and relevance (20%)
  • Q&A performance (10%)

Note: Plagiarism will result in immediate disqualification. All papers will be checked for originality.

Surprise Event

General Rules

  • Team event (2-4 members per team)
  • Event details will be revealed on the day of the symposium
  • All participants registered for other events are eligible
  • No prior preparation required
  • Quick thinking and adaptability are key

What to Expect

  • Challenges will test your engineering knowledge and creativity
  • Time-bound tasks requiring teamwork
  • Problem-solving scenarios
  • Innovation and out-of-the-box thinking

Judging Criteria

  • Problem-solving approach (35%)
  • Teamwork and coordination (30%)
  • Innovation and creativity (25%)
  • Time management (10%)

Note: This is a mystery event! Be ready for anything. The challenge will be revealed only on the event day. Stay tuned!

General Rules for All Events

  • All participants must be college students with valid ID cards
  • Participants must register online before the event
  • Arrive at least 30 minutes before your event starts
  • Dress code: Formal attire (college uniform or formal dress)
  • Mobile phones are not permitted inside the event venue
  • Any form of malpractice will lead to immediate disqualification
  • The decision of the judges and organizing committee is final
  • Participants must follow all instructions given by the event coordinators
  • Certificates will be provided to all participants
  • Prizes will be awarded to top performers in each event

Important: For any queries or clarifications, contact the event coordinators at colosseum@rmkec.ac.in or reach out to the student coordinators.
