CAD Catalog - Rules & Regulations

CAD Catalog

Event Description

  • Individual participation
  • Duration: 35- 45 minutes
  • This event evaluates technical accuracy, drafting speed, and adherence to civil engineering drawing standards through time-bound challenges.
  • Designed for students with strong foundations in technical drawing and digital drafting.

Rules and Regulations

  • Problem statement will be given on the spot
  • The use of mobile phones and other electronic devices (laptops, headphones, smart watches, etc.) Is strictly prohibited
  • The decision of the judges and event coordinators will be final and binding.

Note: Participants must ensure their laptops are fully charged. Power outlets will be available but limited.
