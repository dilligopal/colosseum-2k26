Build the Building - Rules & Regulations

Build the Building

EVENT DISCRIPTION

  • Unleash your inner engineer in the bridge builders challenge 2025! Join us for an exciting, hands-on competition where participants design, construct, and test model bridges for strength, efficiency, and innovation.
  • This event is perfect for aspiring engineers, designers, and creative problem solvers eager to showcase their skills in structural design.

Event Rules and Regulations

  • Team members: 2-3 (max)
  • The use of mobile phones and other electronic devices (laptop, headphones etc...) Is strictly prohibited.
  • The team will be disqualified in case of any violations.

Note: Teams violating any rule will be disqualified immediately. The decision of the judges is final.
