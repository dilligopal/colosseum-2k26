About Us

About us

Colosseum aims to push civil engineering students beyond classroom theory by giving them a competitive platform to prove their technical ability, present original ideas, and solve real-world problems. The symposium brings students from different colleges together to exchange knowledge, test their skills in events like CAD design, model building, paper presentation, and technical quizzes, and develop the kind of practical thinking the industry actually expects.

Colosseum 2k26

Colosseum 2026 is a national-level technical symposium organized by the Department of Civil Engineering at RMK Engineering College. It has consistently been one of the most prominent technical fests in recent years. This edition brings a diverse lineup of events designed to give participants a platform to showcase their full range of skills. With a total of five core events along with exciting hands-on activities, this year's symposium challenges you to step up, compete, and demonstrate what you're capable of.

R.M.K. Engineering College

R.M.K. Engineering College is a premier institution committed to excellence in engineering education. The college provides state-of-the-art facilities and a conducive learning environment for students to excel in their chosen fields. The Department of Civil Engineering is proud to organize Colosseum 2k26, bringing together the best minds in civil engineering from across the nation.
