About Us

Objective

Colosseum aims to push civil engineering students beyond classroom theory by giving them a competitive platform to prove their technical ability, present original ideas, and solve real-world problems. The symposium brings students from different colleges together to exchange knowledge, test their skills in events like CAD design, model building, paper presentation, and technical quizzes, and develop the kind of practical thinking the industry actually expects. It also forces the organizing team to build leadership, coordination, and execution skills rather than just talking about them. Overall, the objective is simple: sharpen technical competence, encourage innovation, and prepare students to function like professionals instead of textbook memorisers.

Colosseum 2k26

Colosseum 2026 is a national-level technical symposium organized by the Department of Civil Engineering at RMK Engineering College. It has consistently been one of the most prominent technical fests in recent years. This edition brings a diverse lineup of events designed to give participants a platform to showcase their full range of skills. With a total of five core events along with exciting hands-on activities, this year’s symposium challenges you to step up, compete, and demonstrate what you’re capable of. Get ready to hustle!
