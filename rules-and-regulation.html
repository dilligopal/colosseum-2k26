Rules and Regulations

R.M.K. Engineering College - Colosseum 2k26 Symposium Rules and Regulations

General Rules and Regulations

Participation Requirements

  • All participants must be currently enrolled students in a recognized engineering college
  • Valid college ID card is mandatory for entry and participation
  • Participants must register online before the event deadline
  • Participation in at least one technical event is mandatory
  • Each participant can register for a maximum of 3 events

Dress Code

  • Formal attire is mandatory for all participants
  • Casual wear, jeans, t-shirts, and sportswear are not allowed
  • Participants not adhering to dress code will be denied entry

Code of Conduct

  • All participants must maintain discipline and decorum throughout the event
  • Respectful behavior towards judges, coordinators, and fellow participants is mandatory
  • Any form of misconduct, harassment, or unruly behavior will result in immediate disqualification
  • Participants must follow all instructions given by event coordinators and volunteers
  • Smoking, consumption of alcohol, or any intoxicating substances is strictly prohibited

Campus Rules

Mobile Phones and Electronic Devices

  • Usage of mobile phones is strictly prohibited inside the event venues
  • Mobile phones must be switched off or kept in silent mode during events
  • Use of mobile phones during competitions will lead to immediate disqualification
  • Electronic devices (except laptops for specific events) are not allowed
  • Photography and videography are allowed only in designated areas

Entry and Exit

  • Participants must arrive at least 30 minutes before their scheduled event
  • Late entries will not be permitted once the event has commenced
  • Participants must report to the registration desk upon arrival
  • Exit from the campus during the event requires permission from coordinators
  • Re-entry after exit may not be permitted

Campus Facilities

  • Participants must maintain cleanliness and hygiene in all campus areas
  • Vandalism or damage to college property will result in strict action
  • Food and Refeshments will be provided
  • Participants must use facilities responsibly and report any issues to coordinators

Competition Rules

Fair Play and Ethics

  • All participants must compete fairly and ethically
  • Any form of cheating, plagiarism, or malpractice will result in immediate disqualification
  • Teams not following the rules will be disqualified without any intimation
  • Use of unauthorized materials or external help is strictly prohibited
  • Participants must submit original work only

Judging and Results

  • The decision of the judges and jury will be final and binding
  • No appeals or disputes regarding results will be entertained
  • Judges reserve the right to disqualify any participant or team for rule violations
  • Results will be announced as per the schedule
  • Certificates will be provided to all participants

Time Management

  • Participants must strictly adhere to the time limits specified for each event
  • No extra time will be granted under any circumstances
  • Participants arriving late will not be given additional time
  • Events will start and end as per the schedule
